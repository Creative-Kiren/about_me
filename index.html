Time for a change

Having worked in HR for 10+ years and when my last employment was made redundant, I decided I needed to take stock of my experience and what skills I can add to HR career. During my career, I always have a natural gravitas towards systems and wanted to expand on this. Having spoken to different friends and colleagues, I realised there is a shortgage of coders. Not having much of an idea what this meant, I did a "little" research and concluded I should start with front-end web development. Who knows, this could lead to a change in career.

So here I am embarking on a new interest. A little scared, frustrated and anxious but my experience in HR has made me resilient so I will be fighting my through to getting it. Watch this space....
